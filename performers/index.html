Our Artists

The Safe Spaces Concert features a diverse lineup of LGBTQ+ artists and allies from across the musical spectrum. Past performers have included Grammy-nominated recording artists, Broadway stars, underground DJs, folk singer-songwriters, and emerging talents making their debut on the legendary Stonewall stage. Every performer shares a commitment to using their platform for positive change and authentic expression.

Interested in performing? We welcome submissions from artists of all genres and experience levels who share our mission. Contact us for audition information and upcoming concert dates.
