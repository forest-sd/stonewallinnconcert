The Concert

The Stonewall Inn Safe Spaces Concert brings together musicians, artists, and advocates to celebrate LGBTQ+ rights and raise awareness about the ongoing need for safe spaces. Held at the historic Stonewall Inn — birthplace of the modern LGBTQ+ rights movement — this event honors the legacy of those who fought for equality while looking forward to the work that remains.

Live Music

Performances by LGBTQ+ artists and allies spanning genres from pop and rock to folk, hip-hop, and classical.

Community

A gathering of people who believe in the power of music to build bridges, heal wounds, and celebrate authentic identity.

Impact

Proceeds support organizations creating safe spaces for LGBTQ+ youth, providing shelter, counseling, and community resources.
