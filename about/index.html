About the Event

Music, history, and the fight for safe spaces.

Why Safe Spaces Matter

For many LGBTQ+ individuals, especially youth, finding a safe space — a place where they can be themselves without fear of judgment, harassment, or violence — remains a daily challenge. The Stonewall Inn Safe Spaces Concert raises awareness and funds for organizations that create these vital environments. From community centers to shelters, crisis hotlines to school programs, safe spaces save lives. The concert honors this mission through the universal language of music, bringing together diverse communities in celebration and solidarity.
